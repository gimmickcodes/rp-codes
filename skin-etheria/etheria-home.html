The Court of Sapphire ›

Split from the same mainland as Diamond and was always the more devout of the two. The usurpation of the old ruling family in Diamond resulted in the death of a saint’s daughter, adding fuel to a centuries-old flame. Tensions were further exacerbated when Diamond disavowed Adornism. Sapphire boasts many river cities sprung up around the beautiful fjords within its borders. Home to Adornism, white marble cathedrals are all over, and many come to marvel at its art and architecture. Sapphire is ruled by a theocratic council of four Saints, and its main focus is spreading Adornism through the seven courts.
