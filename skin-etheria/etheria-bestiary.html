The Amethian Phoenix, a sacred bird in the eyes of Shina, adorns itself with resplendent feathers of gold and purple. Revered for its ethereal allure, this mythical creature has eluded capture for years. Its golden feathers and eggs hint at its Gilded nature, possibly imbued with extraordinary etherial potential. With a mere flap of its wings, the Amethian Phoenix invokes waves of purple flames, mysteriously sparing the forest it calls home while reducing perceived threats to ash. Remarkably resilient, it appears impervious to physical harm, regenerating swiftly from any injuries inflicted upon it. Coveted and sought after, only feathers and egg shells have been retrieved from this elusive creature. Its feathers possess unparalleled regenerative properties, offering rejuvenation and vitality. Similarly, the egg shells found in an abandoned nest were found to have wondrous potential for regeneration.
