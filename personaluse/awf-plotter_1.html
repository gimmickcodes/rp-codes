Carsyn Valentia
20-years old. Single. Demi. Eklekta of Thesphilia
Adopted into the Valentia family at 7, Carsyn is a recent Aegis graduate and military private. He often goes on missions to the Outside as a support role, and also works with the investigations team.

In conversation, he's neither shy nor a loner, but he doesn't actively seek out company. Reserved. Long-suffering. Seemingly impossible to anger. Occasionally blunt, but conflict avoidant. A bit of a pushover. With those he's comfortable with, he has a deadpan humor and slight snarky streak. Is altrusitic, passive aggressive, and prone to worrying. Professionally, he's dutiful to T, but has a tendency to disappear to his own devices when not on duty.

History: Born in Stelaris, Carsyn was the second child of a family of crooks who couldn't afford it. An unplanned kid, his parents would have given him up from the start were it not for two things: one, his seal; and two, the generous compensation Cyphun families were known to provide in exchange for promising eklektas. From then on, their plan was to wait for his powers to pronounce themselves, and then leverage it as best they could to drive up the price.

Present day, Carsyn's taken it to heart that most people—his parents, the Valentias, the army—only want him for what he can do. He's fixated on the idea of being "useful", desperate to be needed in lieu of being liked, and clings to anyone who offers validation outside of that—after he overcomes his initial skepticism.

Hobbies: Wandering around on foot, napping, sketching from life, daydreaming about traveling beyond Absolia, reading about the Old World.
