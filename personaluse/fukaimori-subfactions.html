MOTTO

Brighter than the morning sun

The Imperial Family is the nominal, religious head of the Mori subcontinent. They reside in the palace at Ikyo, the capital of the region.

Once, they were also a centre of militaristic authority, their power waned many years ago and now the land runs as per a feudal system, where the responsibility has been distributed to three major clans who preside over the human occupied regions of the continent.

GOALS

To overtake the yōkai and to reestablish supremacy over the continent, to ensure the welfare of their people and to continue to show people a path of hope.

RELATIONSHIPS

Strong ties with the Fujiwara, Osaki and Towaguchi clans. They are patrons of the arts, crafts and smithery.
